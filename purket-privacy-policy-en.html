Purket Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-08-31

This policy explains how the Purket app (“the App”) handles personal data.

1. Data We Collect

By default, your budgeting data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third party. In this case, the App Store “App Privacy” section may show Data Not Collected.

  • Budget data (pockets, categories, wallets, amounts, notes, etc.): stored on device
  • Accounts/Sign-in: not used
  • Advertising/Tracking: not used

2. Optional Permissions & On‑Device Processing

  • Notifications (optional): used for local reminders such as recurring entries.
  • The App schedules/displays notifications on device only and does not use remote push notifications.
  • No device push tokens are collected or transmitted.
  • You can change or revoke notification permissions in iOS Settings at any time.

The App uses these permissions on-device only and does not transmit data off the device.

3. Third‑Party SDKs

If analytics, crash reporting, or other third‑party SDKs are integrated, data collection may change. We will update this policy and the App Store “App Privacy” details accordingly.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect or store children’s personal information.

5. Data Security & Retention

  • Data is stored locally and is deleted when the App is uninstalled.
  • Depending on your OS/iCloud backup settings, app data may be backed up by Apple; the developer cannot access your backups.

7. Changes
