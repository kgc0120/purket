FAQ

Where is my data stored?

Budget data is stored on your device. Deleting the app will remove the data.

Do you support backup/restore?

Data may be included in your iCloud device backup; the developer cannot access your backups. Export/Import features are planned.

Troubleshooting

  1. Update to the latest app/OS version
  2. Ensure you have enough storage
  3. Restart the app and your device
